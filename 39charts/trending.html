[ How trending works ]
Each song's score starts from its combined views gained per day (NicoNico + YouTube + bilibili, over the most recent week of nightly snapshots). That gain is boosted by momentum — smaller songs growing fast relative to their size rank above giants inching along — and nudged by engagement (likes and comments per 1,000 views). Songs need at least two daily snapshots to qualify, so the list sharpens as more history is collected.
