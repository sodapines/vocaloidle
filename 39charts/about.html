★ What is 39charts?

39charts is a fan-made Vocaloid song rankings site inspired by the classic NicoNicodouga ranking pages. It catalogs and ranks Vocaloid, SynthV, and UTAU songs that have reached significant view milestones on NicoNicodouga and YouTube.

The name "39" comes from the Japanese wordplay 三九 (san-kyu) — "thank you" — a long-standing tradition in the Vocaloid community as a way of expressing gratitude to Hatsune Miku and the creators who built this world.

This site is a companion to Vocaloid Heardle, a daily Vocaloid song guessing game. Both projects are fan-made and not affiliated with Crypton Future Media, Dwango / NicoNico, or any other rights holders.

▸ How are songs ranked?

Songs are ranked by their real view counts. Use the NND / YT / Both toggle on the rankings to rank by NicoNicodouga views, YouTube views, or the two combined (the default). Songs without a live count fall back to their view tier, newest first within a tier.

Each song page shows the full per-platform breakdown — NicoNico views, comments, and mylists and YouTube views, likes, and comments — plus a combined total and an embedded player where the video allows it.

Songs also carry a "Hall" tier based on NicoNico views — Hall of Fame (100K+), Hall of Legends (1M+), Hall of Myths (10M+), Hall of Gods (100M+) — shown alongside the classic milestone badge below.

BadgeMilestoneDescription
100M+ 100 million views All-time legendary status
10M+ 10 million views Major viral hit
1M+ 1 million views Widely popular
100K+ 100,000 views Notable community song
10K+ 10,000 views Emerging song

Live NicoNicodouga and YouTube counts are integrated. Bilibili is a possible future addition.

▸ Data source

Song metadata — titles, producers, vocalists, cover art, view tags, and accepted title variants — comes from VocaDB, a community-maintained database of Vocaloid music. VocaDB data is used under its terms of use.

Cover art is sourced from YouTube and NicoNicodouga via the URLs stored in VocaDB, and vocalist / producer avatars come from VocaDB artist pictures. All rights to artwork belong to their respective creators.
