[ How It Works ]
  1. Find the song on VocaDB and copy its URL or ID.
  2. Paste it below and press Fetch — the details fill in automatically.
  3. Confirm the tier, add an optional note, and submit for review.
  4. The maintainer approves it, and the song joins the rankings.
