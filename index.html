    About

    VOCALOID Heardle is a daily song guessing game built around VOCALOID and vocal synth music.

    Each puzzle reveals a little more of the intro after every wrong guess or skip. Song details and VocaDB links appear after the answer.

    Support

    VOCALOID Heardle is maintained by sodapines as a small fan-made music puzzle project.

    For bugs, song corrections, or suggestions, email kzen@sodapines.dev.

    Hosting and the stats backend cost a bit each month to keep running. If you've enjoyed the game and want to chip in, even a one-time tip helps cover those costs.

    Release Notes

    Release v1.3

    This release collects the major updates being prepared for the public site.

    • Achievement search for filtering by name, description, or category.
    • Local bookmarks with thumbnails, VocaDB links, searchable history, and hosted audio previews.
    • Bookmark audio previews respect the volume setting and show elapsed playback time.
    • Improved bookmark thumbnail fallback handling for low-quality placeholders.
    • Strong-match partial credit for guesses with both the correct producer credit and main vocal synth.
    • Vocal-match partial credit for guesses with the correct main vocal synth.
    • Strong, artist, and vocal matches now use separate purple, yellow, and blue feedback states.
    • Same-title variants can be guessed separately when selected from autocomplete.
